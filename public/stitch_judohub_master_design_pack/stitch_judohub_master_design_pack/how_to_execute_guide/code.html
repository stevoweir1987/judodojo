Tai Otoshi

Green Belt (3rd Kyu) timer 2:45

Key Mechanics

Tai Otoshi is a combination of strong off-balancing (kuzushi), turning (tsukuri) and reaping (kake) the outside leg. It is a hand technique (te-waza) that requires precise timing and leverage.
